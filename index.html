FAQ

Honest questions, honest answers

Will this bot make me money?

We don't know — and you should distrust anyone who says yes. Results depend on the strategy you configure, the market, and your risk management. The bot's edge is execution discipline, not fortune-telling.

Do you show your results?

Yes — real, unedited numbers from the live /stats endpoint, posted with dates on this page and on Telegram as the record builds. Wins and losses both. What we will never do is post cherry-picked screenshots, because past performance — anyone's — doesn't predict yours.

How does the key work? What if I share it?

Your key is personal: it activates your bot, and the invite-only access is tied to YOUR TradingView username. Sharing it kills the access — and honestly, the real value is the updates and support, which only the key owner receives.

Do you have access to my money or API keys?

No. The bot runs on your machine. You create your own API keys, and we recommend creating them WITHOUT withdrawal permission — so not even the bot itself could move funds out.

What do I need?

A Bybit account, TradingView with webhook alerts, a computer that stays on (or a VPS), and about 1 hour with our step-by-step guide. Support via @degenkao.

Refunds?

Membership: cancel anytime, no future charges. Lifetime: because access is delivered immediately, there are no refunds — which is why we ask you to read this entire page first.
